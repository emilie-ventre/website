Spinach Surprise Smoothie


Ingredients:


- 1 cup fresh spinach leaves
- 1 ripe banana, peeled
- 1/2 cup pineapple chunks (fresh or frozen)
- 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
- 1/2 cup almond milk (or any milk of your choice)
- 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup (optional)
- Ice cubes (optional)

Instructions:


1. Wash the spinach leaves thoroughly and drain them.
2. In a blender, combine spinach, banana, pineapple chunks, Greek yogurt, almond milk, and honey/maple syrup if using.
3. Blend until smooth and creamy.
4. If the smoothie is too thick, add more almond milk until desired consistency is reached.
5. Pour into glasses and serve immediately.


Radish Rainbow Refresher


Ingredients:


- 1 cup sliced radishes
- 1 cup chopped cucumber
- 1/2 cup chopped pineapple
- Juice of 1 lime
- 1 tablespoon honey or agave syrup
- 1 cup cold water
- Ice cubes
- Mint leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions:


1. In a blender, combine radishes, cucumber, pineapple, lime juice, honey/agave syrup, and cold water.
2. Blend until smooth.
3. Add ice cubes and blend again until the desired consistency is reached.
4. Pour into glasses, garnish with mint leaves if desired, and serve immediately.


Pineapple Paradise Punch


Ingredients:


- 2 cups chopped pineapple
- 1 ripe banana, peeled
- 1/2 cup coconut milk
- 1/2 cup orange juice
- 1 tablespoon lime juice
- 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup (optional)
- Ice cubes
- Pineapple wedges for garnish (optional)

Instructions:


1. In a blender, combine chopped pineapple, banana, coconut milk, orange juice, lime juice, and honey/maple syrup if using.
2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
3. Add ice cubes and blend again until the desired consistency is reached.
4. Pour into glasses, garnish with pineapple wedges if desired, and serve immediately.


Strawberry Sunshine Sip


Ingredients:


- 1 cup fresh strawberries, hulled
- 1 ripe banana, peeled
- 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
- 1/2 cup orange juice
- 1 tablespoon honey or agave syrup
- Ice cubes
- Fresh strawberries for garnish (optional)

Instructions:


1. In a blender, combine fresh strawberries, banana, Greek yogurt, orange juice, and honey/agave syrup.
2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
3. Add ice cubes and blend again until the desired consistency is reached.
4. Pour into glasses, garnish with fresh strawberries if desired, and serve immediately.


Flower Power Fusion


Ingredients:


- 1 cup mixed edible flowers (such as rose petals, lavender, or hibiscus)
- 1 cup chopped ripe mango
- 1/2 cup coconut water
- Juice of 1 lime
- 1 tablespoon honey or agave syrup
- Ice cubes
- Edible flowers for garnish (optional)

Instructions:


1. In a blender, combine mixed edible flowers, chopped mango, coconut water, lime juice, and honey/agave syrup.
2. Blend until smooth.
3. Add ice cubes and blend again until the desired consistency is reached.
4. Pour into glasses, garnish with edible flowers if desired, and serve immediately.
